Air Jet



¶ Air Jets control the air that goes into the Needle Jet's air bleed. Air Jets come in three flavors.
  1. Screw-in Air Jet.
  2. Pressed-in Air Jet.
  3. No Air Jet.

Air Jets control roughly the same range as the Main Jet. Sort of fine tunes the Main Jet. I doubt that you will be changing it much but you might.
