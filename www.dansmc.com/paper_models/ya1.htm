Yamaha YA1
Yamaha's First Motorcycle

First made in 1955 the Yamaha YA-1 was the first motorcycle produced by the Yamaha Motor Company.
This was the first motorcycle produced in Japan to have a primary kick start system
enabling the engine to be started with the transmission in gear.
