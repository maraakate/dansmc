TT Week Vacation
1932


In 1932 Bert Perryman and his friend Bill Bottin had an absolutely great trip to the Isle of Man TT races. He recounts this trip in his excellent book " A Clubman At Brooklands ". Much to my regret, Bert is no longer with us, but his gracious wife, Mrs. Connie Perryman has given me permission to reprint this great story here.
