SHOP MANUAL


Here is the Shop Manual for an NSU Super Max 250cc. It has a most unusual cam drive. I scanned these in so you could print them out full size ( 8 1/2" by 11" ) so they are, somewhat, big files. To make a copy, you will need to download the page you want (a single .jpg, 100-230kb in size) and save it to a file. Then open the .jpg with a Photo Editing program (I use Corel Photo House 2) and print it from there. Mine came out real nice. If you try to print from the Internet it will not come out right.
